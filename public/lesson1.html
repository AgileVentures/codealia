Lets show some Hello Kitty pictures

Should you accept it - this is your assignment for today:

  1. In the code of the page, please find <settings data-bg="skulls"...> and change "skulls" to some other background. You can choose any of these backgrounds and you may change them as many times as you want: photography, skulls, food, lined_paper, giftly or congruent_pentagon - that's a tricky one!
    Play around with those backgrounds until you are satisfied.
  2. Now, within the <settings> tag, there is another data tag with the text data-tag="hellokitty" in it. Change "hellokitty" to any other word you would like to show pictures about.
  3. Find the <H1> tag on the page. Change the text to how you want to say hello to your visitors.
  4. Close to the <H1> tag you'll find a <H2> tag. Make sure that you change that text as well. You don't want to keep "Hello Kitty" or do you?.
  5. Finally, find the text you are reading right now and delete it or change it to something you want to say to your visitors!
Don't forget to save your work and always ask your teacher if you feel unsure about what to do!
